GENERAL IN FORMATION

The lubrication method is a fully force-fed, full-flow filtration type. The oil pump is a trochoid type which is directly mounted to the crankshaft.

Recommended Precautions The most effective precaution is to adapt working practices which prevent, as far as practicable, the risk of skin contact with mineral oils. For example;

Use enclosed systems for handling used engine oil and degrease components, where practicable, before handling them.

Other precautions:

0 Avoid prolonged and repeated contact with oils, particularly used engine oils.

0 Wear protective clothing, including impervious gloves where practicable.

0 Avoid contaminating clothes, particularly underpants, with oil.

0 Do not put oily rags in pockets, the use of overalls without pockets will avoid this.

0 Do not wear heavily soiled clothing and oil-impregnated foot-wear. Overalls must be cleaned regularly and kept separate from personal clothing.
