CLUTCH PEDAL CHECK AND ADJUSTMENT

1. Turn back the carpet, etc. under the clutch pedal.

2. Measure the clutch pedal height.

Standard value (A):

163.5 - 166.5 mm (6.43 - 6.55 in.)

3. If the height of the clutch pedal is not within the standard value, loosen the lock nut and adjust the pedal height to the standard value using the adjusting bolt (Vehicles without auto-cruise control system) or clutch pedal position switch (Vehicles with auto-cruise control system) or push rod.

Caution Do not push in the master cylinder push rod at this time.
