FUEL USAGE STATEMENT Your car must use unleaded gasoline only.

This car has a fuel filler tube especially designed to accept only the smaller-diameter unleaded gaso- line dispensing nozzle.

Caution Using leaded gasoline in your car will damage the catalytic converter and the oxygen sensor, and affect the warranty coverage validity.

Your car is designed to operate on premium un- leaded gasoline having a minimum octane rating of 87, or 91 RON (Research Octane Number).
