Caution

1. Stop the engine immediately if engine misfiring oc- curs, otherwise an abnormally hot exhaust system will damage the catalytic converter or other under- body parts.

2. Correct and repair the ignition or fuel system if there are malfunctions, otherwise engine misfiring may oc- cur which will damage the catalytic converter.

3. Observe manufacturer’s specifications when doing service work.
