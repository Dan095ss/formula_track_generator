Using a auto-cruise control indicator light

1. Push the main switch to “ON.”

2. With the “SET” switch at the “ON position, turn the ignition switch “ON,” and within one sccond after this, turn the “RESUME” switch to “ON.”

3. Take a reading of a diagnostic trouble code based on the flashing of the auto-cruise control indicator light in the combination meter.
