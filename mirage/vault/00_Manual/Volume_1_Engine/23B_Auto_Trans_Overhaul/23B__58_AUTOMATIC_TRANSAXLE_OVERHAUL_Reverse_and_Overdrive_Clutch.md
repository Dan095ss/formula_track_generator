23B-58 AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE OVERHAUL - Reverse and Overdrive Clutch

' .G+SNAP RING INSTALLATION Check that the clearance between the snap ring and the clutch reaction plate is within the standard value. When measuring the clearance, press the clutch reaction plate by the weight of 49 N (11 Ibs.) evenly. If not within the standard value, select a snap ring to adjust.

Standard value: 1.2 to 1.4 mm (0.0472 to 0.0551 in.)
