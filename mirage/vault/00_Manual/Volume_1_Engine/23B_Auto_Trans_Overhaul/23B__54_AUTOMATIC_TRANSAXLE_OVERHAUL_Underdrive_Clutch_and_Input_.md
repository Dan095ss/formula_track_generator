23B-54 AUTOMATIC TRANSAXLE OVERHAUL - Underdrive Clutch and Input Shaft

-

bD+ SNAP RING INSTALLATION Check that the clearance between the snap ring and the clutch reaction plate is within the standard value.

Standard value:

1.25 to 1.45 mm (0.0492 to 0.0571 in.) cF4A41> 1.65 to 1.85 mm (0.0650 to 0.0728 in.) cF4A42> When measuring the clearance, use the special tool to press the clutch reaction plate evenly. If not within the standard value, select a snap ring to adjust.
