CHARGING SYSTEM

GENERAL INFORMATION 110003684 4

The charging system is a system which charges the battery with the generator output to keep the battery charged at a constant level during varying electrical load.

Operation Rotation of the excited field coil generates AC voltage in the stator.

This alternating current is rectified through diodes to DC voltage having a waveform shown in the illustration at left.

The average output voltage fluctuates slightly with the genera- tor load condition.
