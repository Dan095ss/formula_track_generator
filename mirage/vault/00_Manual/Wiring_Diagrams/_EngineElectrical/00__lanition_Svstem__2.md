SERVICE ADJUSTMENT PROCEDURES

SPARK PLUG CABLE TEST 1104133684 (1) Disconnect, one at a time, each of the spark plug cables while the engine is idling to check whether the engine’s running performance changes or not.

Caution Wear rubber gloves while doing so.

(2) If the engine performance does not change, check the resistance of the spark plug cable, and check the spark plug itself.

SPARK PLUG TEST 110003685

(1) Remove the spark plug and connect to the spark plug cable.

(2) Ground the spark plug outer electrode (body), and crank the engine.

Check to be sure that there is an electrical discharge between the electrodes at this time.
