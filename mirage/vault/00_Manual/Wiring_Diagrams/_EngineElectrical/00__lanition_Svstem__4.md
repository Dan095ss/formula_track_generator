b B + SPARK PLUG CABLE INSTALLATION Improper arrangement of spark plug cables will induce voltage between the cables, causing miss firing and developing a surge at acceleration in high-speed operation. Therefore, be careful to arrange the spark plug cables properly by the follow- ing procedure.

1. Install the spark plug cable clamps as shown in the illustra- tion.

2. The numerals on the support and clamp indicate the spark plug cable No.

3. Pay attention to the following items when the spark plug cables are installed.

(1) Install the cables securely to avoid possible contact with metal parts.

(2) Install the cables neatly, ensuring they are not too tight, loose, twisted or kinked.
