GENERATOR RELAY CONTINUITY CHECK

(I) Remove the generator relay from the relay box inside

the engine compartment.

(2) Use an ohmmeter to check that there is continuity when the (+) terminal of the tester is connected to terminal 4 of the generator relay and the (-) terminal is connected to terminal 2.

(3) Next, check that there is no continuity when the (+)terminal is Connected to terminal 2 and the (-) terminal is connected to terminal 4.

(4) If the continuity checks in steps (2) and (3) show a defect, replace the generator relay.
