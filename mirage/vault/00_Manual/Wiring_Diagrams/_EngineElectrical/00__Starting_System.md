GENERAL INFORMATION I10003674

If the ignition switch is turned to the “START” posi- tion, current flows in the coil provided inside magnet- ic switch, attracting the plunger. When the plunger is attracted, the lever connected to the plunger is actuated to engage the starter clutch.

On the other hand, attracting the plunger will turn on the magnetic switch, allowing the B terminal and M terminal to conduct. Thus, current flows to engage the starter motor.
