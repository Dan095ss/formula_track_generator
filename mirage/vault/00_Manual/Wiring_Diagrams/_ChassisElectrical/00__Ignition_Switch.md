INSPECTION (1) Remove the knee protector, and the column cover.

(2) Disconnect the wiring connector from the ignition switch, and connect an ohmmeter to the switch side connector.

(3) Operate the switch, and check the continuity between the terminals.

(2) Remove the ignition switch mounting screws and pull out

the ignition switch.

(3) Disconnect the connector of the key cylinder switch.

(4) Insert the key into and remove it from the steering lock

cylinder to check for continuity between the terminals.
