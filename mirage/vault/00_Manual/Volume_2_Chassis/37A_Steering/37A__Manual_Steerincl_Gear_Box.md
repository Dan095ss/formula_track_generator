STEERING

- Manual Steerincl Gear Box

DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

+A, RACK SUPPORT COVER REMOVAL

Use the special tool to remove the rack support cover from the gear box.

+B, RACK REMOVAL

Pull out the rack from the gear housing in the direction shown in the illustration.

Caution If the rack is pulled out in the wrong direction, the bushing in the gear box may be damaged by the rack threads.

REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS

.A4 OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

Use the special tool to press the oil seal in the top plug.

A13M0052

.B+ RACK SUPPORT COVER INSTALLATION

Position the rack at its center and tighten the rack support cover to 15 Nm (11 ftlbs.).

.C+TOTAL PINION TORQUE ADJUSTMENT ’ (1) In neutral position, use the special tool to rotate the pinion shaft clockwise one turn per 4 - 6 seconds.

Return the rack support cover 30”

- 60” and adjust the torque to the standard value.

Standard value: 0.3 - 1.4 mm (3 - 12 in. Ibs.) [Torque variation: 0.4 Nm (3 in. Ibs.)]

A13SOO86
