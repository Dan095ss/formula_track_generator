3744-30 STEERING

- Power Steering Gear Box

.H+ OIL SEAL INSTALLATION

Use the special tool to press the oil seal into the valve housing.

The upper surface of the oil seal should project outward approx. 1 mm (.04 in.) from the housing edge surface.

MB990941

Caution If the oil seal is flush with or lower than the housing edge, it will cause oil leaks and require reassembly.

,Jd RACK SUPPORT COVER INSTALLATION

Position the rack at its center. Tighten the rack support cover to 15 Nm (11 ftlbs.).

,K+ TOTAL PINION TORQUE ADJUSTMENT (1)

In neutral position, rotate the pinion shaft clockwise one turn/4 - 6 seconds with the special tool. Return the rack support cover 30” - 60” and adjust torque to the standard value.

Using the special tools, turn the pinion gear at the-rate of one rotation in approximately 4 to 6 seconds to check the total pinion torque.

(2)

Standard value: 0.6 - 1.4 Nm (.4 - 1.0 ft.lbs.) [Change in torque: 0.4 Nm (.3 ftlbs.)]

Caution

1. When adjusting, set the standard value at its highest value.

2.

Assure no ratcheting or catching when operating the rack toward the shaft direction.

006

NOTE When it cannot be adjusted within the specified return angle, check or replace the rack support cover components.

(3) After adjusting, lock the rack support cover with lock nut.
