Standard value: - 0”40’+30’ (The difference between the left and right wheels should be 30’ or less.)

NOTE For vehicles equipped with aluminum wheels, measure the camber after tightening the special tool (MB991004) to the specified torque 172 Nm (127 ftlbs.).

Caution Never subject the wheel bearings to the full vehicle load when the flange nuts are loosened.
