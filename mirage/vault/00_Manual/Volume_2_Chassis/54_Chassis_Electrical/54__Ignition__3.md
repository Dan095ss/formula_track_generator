INSPECTION 54300220117

IGNITION SWITCH CONTINUITY CHECK

1.

Remove the column cover lower and upper.

2.

Disconnect the wiring connector from the ignition switch and connect an ohmmeter to the switch side connector.

3.

Operate the ignition switch and check the continuity.
