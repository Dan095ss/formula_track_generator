EXTERIOR A Front Bumper .

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

+A, RADIATOR GRILLE REMOVAL <2-door models>

Remove the radiator grille by pushing the tab of the radiator grille clips in the direction of the arrows with a fiat-tipped screwdriver, while lightly pulling the radiator grille towards you.
