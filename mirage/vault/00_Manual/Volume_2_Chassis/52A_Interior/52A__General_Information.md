OPERATION

Seat belt warning buzzer

When the ignition switch is turned ON, the deci- sion circuit operates and the seat belt warning light inside of the combination meter flashes four times for 6 seconds.

The decision circuit output makes the seat belt warning buzzer sound intermittently for 6 se- conds to indicate unfastening of the seat belt.
