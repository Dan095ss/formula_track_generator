REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION

1. Inside rear view mirror

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

+A,INSIDE REAR VIEW MIRROR REMOVAL

Insert a narrow flat-tip screwdriver into the slit in the inside rear view mirror bracket, keep the spring pushed in and remove the inside rear view mirror in the direction of the arrow in the illustration.

NOTE While the spring is pushed in, the connection between the spring and the paw1 of the button is released.
