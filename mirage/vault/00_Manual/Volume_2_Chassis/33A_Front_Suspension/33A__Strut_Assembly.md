FA( SELF-LOCKING NUT INSTALLATION

(1) With the coil spring held compressed by the special tools (MB991237 and MB991238), provisionally tighten the self-locking nut.

Caution Do not use an impact wrench to tighten the special tool bolt.

(2) Line up the holes in the strut assembly spring lower seat with the hole in the spring upper seat.

NOTE The alignment is easily accomplished with a rod.

(3) Correctly align both ends of the coil spring with the grooves in the spring seat, and then loosen the special tools (MB991237 and MB991238).
